# Code of Conduct

## 1. Statement on Diversity and Inclusivity

We are committed to fostering a welcoming, diverse, and inclusive environment for everyone, regardless of gender, sexual orientation, disability, physical appearance, body size, race, or religion. We expect all team members and contributors to treat each other with respect and professionalism.

## 2. Expected and Unacceptable Behaviour

**Expected Behaviour:**

- Be respectful and considerate of differing viewpoints and experiences.
- Gracefully accept constructive criticism.
- Focus on what is best for the team and the project.
- Communicate collaboratively and openly.

**Unacceptable Behaviour:**

- The use of sexualized language or imagery and unwelcome sexual attention or advances.
- Trolling, insulting/derogatory comments, and personal or political attacks.
- Public or private harassment.
- Publishing others' private information, such as a physical or electronic address, without explicit permission.

## 3. Consequences of Unacceptable Behaviour

Unacceptable behavior from any team member or contributor will not be tolerated. Consequences for violating this code of conduct may include:

- A formal warning from the team.
- Temporary or permanent exclusion from team meetings and project repositories.
- Reporting the incident to the course instructors (DSCI 310 Instructional Team) for further academic or disciplinary action.

## 4. Reporting and Enforcement Procedure

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, or have any other concerns, please report it by:

1. Reaching out directly to the individual involved to resolve the issue amicably if you feel comfortable doing so.
2. If the issue persists or is severe, notifying the rest of the team members immediately.
3. If the issue cannot be resolved internally within the group, it must be reported to the DSCI 310 instructional team via email or a private message on the course communication platform. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated, and will result in a response that is deemed necessary and appropriate to the circumstances.
